About

For fifteen years, experienced Villa Bella Salon hair stylist Maria Haq has been beautifying San Antonio, one haircut and color at a time. Beginning each visit with a personalized hair consultation, Maria will work with you to discover your individual style. Issues like your best facial features, how much styling time you have in the morning, and other important information will all be gained prior to cutting or dyeing to ensure your new do makes you look your best and feel your best. Up to date on all the latest hair tricks and techniques, Maria can apply hair extensions, cut, and style your hair using a precise and delicate hand.
